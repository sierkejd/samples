### Reviewing the Route
1. Navigate to `Routes > Active Route`.

### Exporting Track History
1. Insert USB drive into the console port.
2. Go to `Tools > Export Track Log`.
3. Select the desired date range.
4. Click **Export** and safely eject the drive.
